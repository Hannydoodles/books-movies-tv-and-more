TV Shows

Both of us enjoy watching TV. We have loads of shows that we love. Here are just a few of our favourites.

The Flash

This is a show all about a forensic scientist, who is secretly the fastest man alive, and saves people using the name Flash. With the help of his friends at STAR Labs, he fights crime and finds other Meta Humans like himself.

The Flash is a really fun and enjoyable show that both of us adore. It is very funny, and even the villans are lovable.

We rate this show 9.5 out of 10.

Once Upon a Time

This is a show all about a place named Story Brook. This place is a little different from everywhere else; everyone who lives there is from a book. They had been trapped in the real world by the evil queen, Regina. A prophesy exists, stating that Emma Swan is the only one who can save them all.

Manraj absoloutly loves this show. Hannah has only just started watching series 1, but is already intrigued by its interesting story.

We rate this show an 8 out of 10.

Supernatural

This is a show all about brothers, Sam and Dean Winchester, who are hunters. This means that they fight things like Vampires, Werewolves, Demons and more to try and save the lives of people across America. They eventually meet an angel named Castiel, and he joins the team.

This is one of Hannah's favourite shows to watch. She loves its humour and the way that it can be so fun and yet so heartfelt at the same time.

We rate this show 9 out of 10.

Stranger Things

Stranger things is a mystery/thriller show, where nothing is quite as it seems. The series starts with a boy called Will Buyers going missing. His friends and family try to find him, and in the process they meet a multitude of scary and strange things.

This is Hannah's favourite show ever! Manraj hasnt seen it but wants to and will soon.

We rate this show a 9.5 out of 10.
